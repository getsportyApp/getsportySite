There are different parametres to measure the success of any country in any given field. For some countries winning a Gold medal at the Olympics or lifting a prestigious trophy in the final of any global showpiece event is the only evaluation of their success in the event. While for some countries a mere participation in a prestigious tournament is an honor and is termed as a remarkable achievement. For example- For Brazil, a Soccer mad country, a World Cup is a big failure if their soccer stars don't bring that coveted trophy to Rio de Janerio and for a country like India, an appearance in the FIFA World Cup will be considered as a huge victory because of our country's absence in the FIFA World Cup through out its football histroy.

Here we are taking "Population and the Medals" as the parameter to measure how successful a country has been in the Olympics. We have compiled a list of all the countries that have participated in the summer Olympic games since 1896. So according to our list, Nordic countries like Finland, Sweden, Denmark and Norway have fared far better than the traditional sports powerhouses like USA, Great Britain and Russia.
